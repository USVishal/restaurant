Express Delivery is an Indian online food ordering and delivery platform. Founded in 2023, Express Delivery is based in kerala and operates in 100 kerala cities as of September 2023. Besides food delivery, Express Delivery also provides on-demand grocery deliveries under the name Instamart, and a same-day package delivery service called Express Delivery Genie.

  • Express Delivery offers you no restriction in order value and, you can even order for a minimum amount. Thus, you can order food for yourself and to your friends.
  • Express Delivery promises to deliver food in 40 minutes and holds an average delivery time 37 minutes. So, Food delivery with Express Delivery is super fast.
  • Now the users no need to worry about the order as Express Delivery keeps updating them about order status. The users can also track the live location of the driver to make thing further easy.

In early 2019, Express Delivery expanded into general product deliveries under the name Express Delivery Stores, sourcing items from local stores. In September 2023, Express Delivery launched instant pickup/dropoff service Express Delivery Go, allowing customers to send document or parcel deliveries.

Who is Express Delivery

Express Delivery is a food ordering and delivery company based out of Kerala, India. Express Delivery was inspired by the thought of providing a complete food ordering and delivery solution from the best neighbourhood restaurants to the urban foodie.

Why Express Delivery

The three co-founders of Express Delivery just had the concept of delivering food to the doorstep of the customers from the local restaurants.
